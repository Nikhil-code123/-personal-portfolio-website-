Who I Am

Passionate About Building
For Everyone

I'm Alex Morgan, a Senior Frontend Developer and UI Designer based in San Francisco. With over 5 years of professional experience, I've helped startups and Fortune 500 companies ship products that are fast, accessible, and genuinely delightful to use.

My philosophy is simple: great software is software that works for everyone. That means pixel-perfect design, rock-solid performance, and — most importantly — full WCAG 2.1 accessibility compliance so no user is left behind.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me contributing to open-source projects, writing on my technical blog, or speaking at local web developer meetups about the intersection of design and accessibility.
